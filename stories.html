Stories of Changes

Real Lives, Real Transformations

Inspiring Journeys of Transformation

These are stories of real individuals whose lives have been transformed through IDENTITY Foundation's programs. Each story represents hope, determination, and the power of education, skill development, and community support. Through our career counseling, skill training, and holistic support, we've helped thousands of young people and women build better futures for themselves and their families.

From Confusion to Clarity: A Student's Career Journey

Ravi was confused about his future after school, unaware of the diverse career paths available to him. Through our Career Counseling program, he received guidance on multiple options, explored his aptitude, and gained clarity on his strengths. Today, he's studying engineering with a clear purpose and confidence.

- Empowered Through Career Guidance

From Unemployment to Employment: Digital Marketing Success

Priya was unemployed for two years after completing her schooling. Our Skilling Program trained her in Digital Marketing, and with ongoing support and job placement assistance, she now works with a leading digital agency, earning a respectable income and contributing to her family's welfare.

- Empowered Through Skill Development

Building Confidence: A Rural Woman's Transformation

Anjali, a rural woman facing financial hardship and low confidence, participated in our Mental Health and Livelihood Program. Through counseling and skills training in Business Process Outsourcing, she gained emotional resilience and secured employment, becoming an inspiration to women in her community.

- Strength Through Mental Health & Livelihood Support

From Student to NDA Officer: Preparation Excellence

Arjun dreamed of joining the military but lacked proper guidance for NDA entrance. Our specialized NDA Coaching Program provided comprehensive preparation, and with dedication and our support, he successfully cleared the NDA examination and is now serving the nation as an officer.

- Success Through Competitive Coaching
