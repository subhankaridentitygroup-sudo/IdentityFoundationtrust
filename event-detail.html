Health Camp

Free health checkup and medicine distribution for local communities.

10 Feb 2026
Community Hall, Bhubaneswar
Open to all community members

About this Event

Join our health camp for a day of free screenings, medical consultations, and medicine distribution. The event brings together local medical professionals, healthcare volunteers, and community members to help ensure better access to primary health services.

Key Benefits

  • Free general health checkups and vital screenings.
  • Access to expert consultations from doctors and nurses.
  • Distribution of free medicines and wellness advice.
  • Follow-up assistance and referrals for further care.

Event Overview

This event is designed to deliver preventative care and raise awareness about common health issues in underserved neighbourhoods. Participants can receive blood pressure checks, glucose screenings, and general health counselling at no cost.
