Career Counselling

Enhancing Strategic Decision-Making

Our Flagship Career Guidance Initiative

Our flagship guidance initiatives ensure that students gain clear awareness of multiple career options compared to the limited information available to them earlier. IDENTITY Foundation's Career Counselling program provides comprehensive guidance to help students and professionals make informed career decisions, explore opportunities, and achieve their professional goals.

Measurable Outcomes of Strategic Counseling

Verified Career Clarity

Students receive verified clarity on diverse career paths, enabling informed decision-making for their future.

Increased Peer Discussions

Peer group discussions increase due to the additional career information provided, fostering collaborative learning.

Boosted Self-Esteem

Boosted self-esteem and confidence, especially among girls, empowering them to pursue their aspirations.

Financial Aid Awareness

Improved understanding of financial aid and scholarship opportunities to overcome economic barriers.

Our Counselling Services

  • One-on-one career assessment and counseling sessions
  • Aptitude testing and career path identification
  • Resume and interview coaching
  • Job and internship placement support
  • Higher education guidance
  • Professional development workshops
  • Financial aid and scholarship information

Program Impact

  • Students gain clear awareness of multiple career options
  • Enhanced decision-making capacity for career planning
  • Increased confidence and self-belief among participants
  • Better understanding of educational pathways
  • Access to financial aid and scholarship opportunities
  • Improved college and career selection outcomes
