About the Founder

Leading With Vision, Purpose, and National Impact

Mr. Jasobant Narayan Singhlal, Founder of IDENTITY Foundation, is one of India's most respected voices in career counselling, employability training, and youth empowerment, with over 17 years of dedicated work across the education and skilling ecosystem.

A postgraduate in English and a Ph.D. scholar in Literature at VSSUT, Burla, he combines strong academic grounding with globally recognised professional credentials - he is a BEC-certified trainer from the British Council and an International Meta-NLP Certified Practitioner from the Neuro Semantics Society, USA.

His work carries a rare mark of institutional trust: he is empanelled as a Master Career Counsellor and Consultant with UNICEF, the RBI, OSEPA, and the Department of Higher Secondary Education, Government of Odisha - a testament to the credibility and quality that underpin IDENTITY Foundation's own government and institutional partnerships. Through this work, he has personally guided career counselling initiatives reaching over 22 lakh students across 30 districts of Odisha.

Beyond the classroom, he has trained and spoken at some of the country's most respected institutions and corporations - including Madhusudan Academy (OAS & OFS), Indian Oil, OPTCL, Tata Power, TCS, Power Grid Corporation, GMR, and Emami - and is a recognised media voice on career and youth development, featured on 92.7 Big FM, All India Radio, OTV, Naxatra News, and News World Odisha.

It is this foundation of institutional trust, national reach, and grassroots commitment that he now channels into IDENTITY Foundation - extending the same mission of career guidance, skill development, and mental wellbeing to underserved communities, in active alignment with NEP 2020 and India's broader development goals.

"His mission remains constant: to empower youth with clarity and confidence for meaningful careers, and to nurture educators who can shape the next generation."

Our Mission

To transform lives through education, career guidance, skill development, mental wellbeing, teacher empowerment, and community outreach while extending care and support to vulnerable children, senior citizens, and underserved communities through meaningful social initiatives and partnerships.

Our Vision

To create an equitable and compassionate society where every individual has the opportunity to learn, grow, thrive, and contribute meaningfully to the community.

Our Philosophy

We believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed. By combining education, career guidance, skill development, mental wellbeing, and compassionate community outreach, we empower people to realize their potential, lead lives of dignity, and contribute positively to society. Our philosophy is simple: empower people, strengthen communities, and create lasting social impact.

Our Core Methodology

Educate & Empower

We believe knowledge is the foundation of transformation. Through career guidance, life skills education, teacher capacity building, mental health awareness, and skill development programmes, we empower individuals to make informed decisions, build confidence, and unlock their full potential.

Skill & Enable

We equip individuals with industry-relevant technical, digital, and employability skills that prepare them for meaningful careers and sustainable livelihoods. Our experiential learning approach combines practical training, mentorship, workplace readiness, and placement support to bridge the gap between education and employment.

Care & Transform

We believe sustainable development is built on compassion and inclusion. Through community outreach, charitable initiatives, mental wellbeing programmes, and collaborative partnerships, we work to improve the lives of vulnerable populations while fostering empathy, dignity, and social responsibility.
