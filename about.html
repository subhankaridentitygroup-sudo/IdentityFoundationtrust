About IDENTITY Foundation

Empowering Communities With Care, Clarity, and Commitment

Our Mission

To transform lives through education, career guidance, skill development, mental wellbeing, teacher empowerment, and community outreach while extending care and support to vulnerable children, senior citizens, and underserved communities through meaningful social initiatives and partnerships.

Our Vision

To create an equitable and compassionate society where every individual has the opportunity to learn, grow, thrive, and contribute meaningfully to the community.

Our Philosophy

We believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed. By combining education, career guidance, skill development, mental wellbeing, and compassionate community outreach, we empower people to realize their potential, lead lives of dignity, and contribute positively to society. Our philosophy is simple—empower people, strengthen communities, and create lasting social impact.

Our Core Methodology

Educate & Empower

We believe knowledge is the foundation of transformation. Through career guidance, life skills education, teacher capacity building, mental health awareness, and skill development programmes, we empower individuals to make informed decisions, build confidence, and unlock their full potential.

Skill & Enable

We equip individuals with industry-relevant technical, digital, and employability skills that prepare them for meaningful careers and sustainable livelihoods. Our experiential learning approach combines practical training, mentorship, workplace readiness, and placement support to bridge the gap between education and employment.

Care & Transform

We believe sustainable development is built on compassion and inclusion. Through community outreach, charitable initiatives, mental wellbeing programmes, and collaborative partnerships, we work to improve the lives of vulnerable populations while fostering empathy, dignity, and social responsibility.
