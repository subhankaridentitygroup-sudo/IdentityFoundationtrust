Skilling Mental Health (Women) Career Counselling

Skilling

Industry-relevant skills for meaningful employment and sustainable livelihoods

Why It Is Needed ?

Despite completing their education, many young people remain unemployed due to a mismatch between academic learning and industry requirements. Limited access to career guidance, practical training, digital skills, and workplace exposure prevents them from securing meaningful employment. Our skilling programmes bridge this gap by equipping youth with industry-relevant skills, enhancing their employability, and creating pathways to sustainable livelihoods.

Who Needs This ?

Our programmes are designed for unemployed youth, graduates, school and college pass-outs, and individuals from economically weaker and marginalized communities who aspire to build successful careers. We focus on empowering young people with the skills, confidence, and opportunities needed to thrive in today's competitive job market.

Objectives:

  • Develop industry-relevant technical, digital, and employability skills aligned with current workforce demands.
  • Enhance communication, personality development, and workplace readiness for successful career transitions.
  • Provide career guidance, hands-on training, and placement support to improve employment outcomes.
  • Promote sustainable livelihoods and socio-economic empowerment by connecting skilled youth with meaningful employment opportunities.

Mental Health (Women)

Emotional wellbeing, resilience, and stigma-free support for girls and young women

Why It Is Needed ?

Adolescent girls and young women often face unique emotional and psychological challenges arising from academic pressure, career uncertainty, family expectations, peer influence, relationship concerns, and the growing impact of social media. Despite these challenges, mental health issues frequently remain unaddressed due to stigma, limited awareness, and inadequate access to support. This programme promotes mental health awareness, emotional resilience, and healthy coping strategies, enabling girls to lead healthier, more confident, and fulfilling lives.

Who Needs This ?

This programme is specially designed for girls studying from Class 10 to Graduation in schools, colleges, and educational institutions. It supports adolescent girls and young women during a crucial stage of their personal, academic, and emotional development, while also engaging parents and teachers to foster a safe, supportive, and stigma-free environment for mental wellbeing.

Counselling & Emotional Support:

The programme offers professional counselling, emotional wellbeing workshops, and guided group discussions facilitated by trained counsellors and mental health professionals. Girls receive practical guidance to manage stress, strengthen emotional resilience, improve self-esteem, enhance communication and decision-making skills, and develop healthy coping mechanisms. Safe and inclusive spaces encourage participants to openly express their concerns, seek support, and build positive mental wellbeing.

Objectives:

  • Promote mental health awareness and emotional wellbeing among girls from Class 10 to Graduation.
  • Equip girls with practical strategies to manage exam stress, career pressure, family expectations, peer influence, relationship concerns, and emotional challenges.
  • Build confidence, self-awareness, emotional resilience, and communication through counselling and interactive learning.
  • Create supportive school and community environments by engaging parents, teachers, and stakeholders in promoting positive mental health and reducing stigma.

Career Counselling

Personalized guidance for informed academic and career choices

Why It Is Needed ?

Choosing the right career is one of the most important decisions in a student's life, yet many students lack awareness of career opportunities, higher education pathways, scholarships, and emerging professions. Limited access to personalized guidance often leads to confusion, uninformed decisions, and missed opportunities. Our Career Guidance Programme empowers students with the knowledge, confidence, and direction needed to make informed academic and career choices.

Who Needs This ?

This programme is designed for students of Classes 9-12 students, parents, and educators who seek structured career planning and guidance. It particularly benefits young learners exploring higher education options, competitive examinations, scholarships, and future career pathways.

Psychometric Assessment:

  • Scientific Career Assessment: Evaluates an individual's aptitude, personality, interests, values, learning style, and goal orientation using standardized assessment tools.
  • Personalized Career Insights: Identifies strengths, potential, and suitable career pathways based on individual profiles rather than assumptions.
  • Informed Academic & Career Decisions: Helps students choose the right stream, course, college, and career aligned with their abilities and aspirations according to the career cluster.
  • One-to-One Expert Counselling: Assessment results are interpreted by trained career counsellors, who provide personalized guidance and an actionable career roadmap.
  • Confidence in Career Planning: Enables students and parents to make well-informed decisions, reducing confusion and increasing clarity about future opportunities.
  • Career Dashboard: Students receive access to an online dashboard containing their psychometric report, descriptive career library, career pathways, institutions, entrance exams, scholarships, abroad study consultancy, and other career resources for anytime, anywhere access.

Objectives:

  • Help students discover their aptitude, interests, personality, and strengths through scientific career assessments.
  • Enable informed career decisions by providing guidance on courses, colleges, universities, entrance examinations, scholarships, and emerging careers.
  • Build confidence, decision-making abilities, and career readiness through personalized counselling, mentoring, career awareness programmes, and psychometric assessment.
  • Promote equitable access to career opportunities by connecting students with the right educational and professional pathways for long-term success.
